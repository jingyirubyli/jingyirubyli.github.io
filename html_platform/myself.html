About Me

I have arrived in Tokyo for one month and a half. In the first place, the courses I am taking are interesting and I can learn some important and useful skills from them. Secondly,the people I met are kind and friendly. They are ready for helping others both in study and in daily life. I like to be friends with them. What's more, Tokyo is a beautiful city. I would like to go out to appreciate the scenery and go shopping on my own or with friends. In the future, I want to go to Osaka, Hokkaido, Kyoto and so on. I will never forget the experience in Tokyo.
